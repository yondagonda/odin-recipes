Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 c. (1 stick) butter, softened
  • 1 baguette or French bread, cut lengthwise
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp. freshly chopped parsley
  • 1/4 c. grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â° and line a large baking sheet with foil. Make garlic compound butter: In a small bowl, mix softened butter with garlic, salt, and parsley until well combined.
  2. Spread cut sides of bread liberally with garlic butter. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  3. Bake until bread is toasty and golden at the edges, about 10 minutes.
